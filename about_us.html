About Us

Welcome to Gepukholic, where bold flavors and authentic taste come together.

So Spicy, So Hot

Gepukholic was founded with a passion for bringing the authentic taste of Ayam Gepuk to a modern audience.Inspired by traditional Indonesian flavours, we focus on fresh ingredients, bold sambal, and a signature gepuk technique that brings out rich, satisfying taste in every bite. Today, Gepukholic continues to serve flavour-packed meals that celebrate tradition with a contemporary touch.

Our Vision

To become a leading brand in serving authentic Ayam Gepuk by delivering bold flavours, consistent quality, and unforgettable dining experiences.

Our Mission

  • To serve high-quality food using fresh and authentic ingredients
  • To preserve the traditional taste of Ayam Gepuk with a modern twist
  • To provide excellent service and a satisfying customer experience
  • To continuously innovate while respecting our culinary heritage
